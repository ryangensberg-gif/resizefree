About ResizeFree

ResizeFree is a bundle of fast, privacy-first tools for images and PDFs—right in your browser. Resize, compress, crop, flip, rotate, convert between PNG/JPG/WebP, make memes, pick colors, and convert images ↔ PDF without uploading files to a server. Everything runs locally on your device.

We built this to help students, hobbyists, creators, and small teams finish their projects without us taking any money—no paywalls, no signup. Use it freely for schoolwork, portfolios, pitches, and client mockups. If it helps your project succeed, that’s our win.

  • Private by design: files stay on your device.
  • Free to use: no accounts or subscriptions.
  • Works on modern browsers on desktop and mobile.
